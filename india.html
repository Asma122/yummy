Biryani

It is nice to diversify our dishes and look at the cultures and foods of other peoples and 
quote them for knowledge and pleasure. We offer you the easiest and best recipes for my lady to prepare
a delicious and delicious dish of Indian biryani

Ingredients:

1.	Chicken: chicken (cut into 4 sections)
2.	Biryani dressing for chicken:
3.	Milk: a cup
4.	Green Coriander: 5 tablespoons (finely chopped).
5.	Mint: 3 tablespoons (fine cut).
6.	Onions: cup (reddish slices).
7.	Garlic: 7 cloves (crushed).
8.	Ginger: 3 tablespoons (crushed)
9.	Lemon juice: half a grain
10.	Corn oil: 3 tablespoons
11.	Salt: a teaspoon
12.	Black Pepper: Half a teaspoon
13.	Cinnamon: a teaspoon.
14.	Hill: a teaspoon
15.	Cloves: 1 teaspoon (ground)
16.	Biryani Spices: A teaspoon (or sharp Gramsala spices)
17.	Basmati rice: 2 cups
18.	The ingredients of basmati rice:
19.	Bay leaf: 2 sheets
20.	Cinnamon stick
21.	Cloves: a quarter of a teaspoon
22.	Hill:  a quarter of a teaspoon
23.	Lome: a quarter of a teaspoon
24.	Salt: a quarter of a teaspoon
25.	Chicken broth: 1 cube
26.	Mint: quarter cup (green)
27.	Onions:quarter cup (roasted wings or golden brown color)
28.	Obesity: two tablespoons
29.	Rose Water: 1 tablespoon
30.	Saffron: sprinkle

Steps to Make It:

1.	We wash the chicken well and cut it 4 pieces. And it is possible to remove
        the skin.Bentbel chicken seasoning and leave drenched for an hour.
2.	With a saucepan, put two spoonfuls of butter and corn oil, put the spiced chicken, 
        and put the chicken in it with all the marinade. And stir until the chicken takes 
        a golden color. And fried for 10 minutes. Then immerse it in water by two large cups.
3.	Bring it to a boil until the chicken is leveled for half an hour
4.	Let the chicken level and dry its water until a little remains. Then we keep the
        chicken in the pot and put the rice on top, or remove the chicken from the pot and place
        it in the oven tray and grill.
5.	To prepare the rice: we wash the rice well, prepare a cooker with hot water and put bay leaves, 
6.	cinnamon sticks, cloves, cardamom, lome, salt and a possible Maggie cube. And when it 
         boils, drop the rice in it soaked and boil it for 10 minutes over high heat, taking into
         account that the amount of water is more than the rice until it is kneaded. When 10 minutes
         have passed, we filter it with a strainer and place it over the biryani seasoning with the
         chicken broth in the second saucepan (in which we boiled the chicken with the marinade).
7.	Place a layer of rice on top of it, sprinkle with green coriander, with a green mint leaf and
         a little seared onion. Then a layer of rice, then coriander, mint and burned onions, and put saffron
         with a quarter cup of small water and a tablespoon of rose water and sprinkle on the rice to take a
         golden color and do not stir the rice, just let it be steamed. Cover well for half an hour on low heat
         and serve with chicken.

Gulab Jamun

     If you like Indian cuisine then you should try Gulab Jamun.

Ingredients:

1.	Dough:
2.	Powdered milk, 1 large cup
3.	Half a cup of flour
4.	Soda half a teaspoon
5.	Melted butter 2 tbsp
6.	Sugar syrup :
7.	Sugar, 2 cups
8.	Water 1 cup
9.	Vegetable oil for frying

Steps to Make It:

1.	Mix the milk powder with butter, then add flour and soda until we have a cohesive dough.
2.	Cut the dough into equal parts from 18-20 parts, then place each part between the hands of the hand
        until we have medium sized smooth balls.
3.	Describe the balls in a dish, cover with a dry cloth and leave aside (until the dough is fermented).
4.	The oil is heated in a pan, then the candy balls are placed in the oil and fried over medium heat. 
        (You should not move the candy balls while frying in order not to break.)
5.	Gently move the frying pan when frying until the balls are brown on all sides.
6.	After 5 minutes the candy balls will start floating on the pan.
7.	Dessert balls are dipped in sugar syrup and then placed in a serving dish. Prepare sugar syrup
8.	You must prepare the sugar syrup before you start preparing the "Gulab Jamun" dessert, so that
        it is cold.
9.	Mix sugar with water and put in a bowl over medium heat and stir with a spoon for 10 minutes until
        sugar dissolves in water.
10.	Sugar syrup is placed in a deep bowl aside to cool.

Dahi puri

Indian Dahi Puri is a distinctive recipe for Indian food, and the ingredients provided 
in this recipe are enough for four people. Dahi Puri is prepared by Naya Restaurant - Jumeirah 
Beach Hotel, which has been dubbed, Anandara Puri with Milk in a private Naya way, the puree can
be bought ready (20 Piece) or can be prepared at home.

Ingredients:

Puree:
1.	Wheat flour (two cups each 250 ml)
2.	Oil (two tablespoons of dough)
3.	Semolina (five tablespoons)
4.	Water (three quarters of a cup with a capacity of 250 ml)
5.	frying oil
6.	Salt (fist or as desired)
Filling:
1.	Boiled chickpeas (200 g)
2.	Chopped ginger (10 grams)
3.	Masala Chat (5 grams)
4.	Salt (5 grams)
5.	Whole milk (400 g)
6.	Tamarind sauce (100 g).
7.	Mint Sauce (100 g)
8.	Ripe Pomegranate Seeds (50 g)

Steps to Make It:

Puree:
1.	Soak the semolina in water for 5 minutes.
2.	Dry it with water and set aside. Put the wheat flour in a bowl and bring the salt to it,
        then add oil and semolina and mix them with a spatula spoon that comes with long holes by scraping
        the edges and edges. Wet semolina will moisten the flour so be careful while adding water. The dough
        should be firmly kneaded until smooth. Drip a little oil and cover it with a damp cloth and leave it
        aside for 30 minutes.
3.	Spread the dough into small balls (about the size of a golf ball), then sprinkle a little flour on the
        rim and knead it into circles of 7.5 cm in diameter.
4.	Heat enough oil on the pan to fry the puré (preferably in the form of a Chinese wok pan or Indian kadai
        frying pan). The oil should be hot enough as the dough starts to buzz once in contact with the oil. 
        Add a little salt to the oil to stop the smoke, then fry the puré separately. Dip the first puree and
        gently squeeze it using a tablespoon. As you press it, you will notice its swelling, so constantly flip
        it over once the light brown color appears on it. After preparing the puree, remove it from the oil and
        put it on absorbent paper to absorb the extra oil from it.
Filling:
5.	In a mixing bowl, crush chickpeas, ginger, green pepper, salt and chat masala with your fingertips
        or whatever tool suits you.
6.	Make an opening in the pastries you previously attended and fill them with hummus mixture.
7.	Add sugar to the milk until smooth and cool. Meanwhile add mint sauce and tamarind sauce to pastries.
8.	Place sweet milk on top of pastries and garnish with coriander and pomegranate seeds.
9.	Put 4 grains on each plate and serve it as soon as it is ready to keep the dough fresh and be sure to
        eat it all at once until you enjoy the full taste once

Aloo tikki

As with most countries in the world, there is a fondness for potatoes inside India.
Aloe tiki is one of the most popular gat dishes nationwide. In fact, it is almost impossible
to receive a cart to sell "chat" in India, the menu is empty of "chat", and the dish consists
mainly of boiled potatoes mixed with spices and lentils, and it is being browned.Sometimes, GAT
is filled with a spiced mixture of nuts. It is also decorated with a variety of sweet and sour sauce

Ingredients:

1.	kilo of potatoes.
2.	1/4 cup vegetable oil.
3.	2 green chili peppers, chopped
4.	3 tablespoons of fresh minced coriander
5.	2 tablespoons ginger garlic and crushed garlic powder.
        Available in ginger garlic-paste supermarket.
6.	Salt, to taste .
7.	Half a tablespoon of Gramasala
8.	3 quarters of a tablespoon of cumin powder

Steps to Make It:

1.	Climb the potatoes in a saucepan over the heat until ripe, then grate and place in a bowl.
2.	Add salt, cumin, green pepper, and ginger to the potatoes and mix well.
3.	The mixture is formed in the form of balls and then simplified between the hands of the hands.
4.	Heat the oil in a non-stick frying pan and flat on a fire, then fry the potato mixture pieces
        until they turn golden brown in color on both sides.
5.	Serve hot with tamarind sauce or with yoghurt mixed with turmeric sprinkle.
6.	You can serve in burger bun by adding tomatoes, lettuce and onion slices.

Samosa

Samosas is one of the most famous types of pastries in the world, as it is presented as
a side dish in wills. Samosas are distinguished by their delicious taste and beautiful shape.
Samosa pastry and stuffing, where there is chicken samosa, potato samosa, and samosa with Indian
filling,and in this article we will learn how to prepare Indian samosa.

Ingredients:

To prepare the dough:
1.	Flour - 2 cups
2.	Water - as needed
3.	Salt - two tablespoons
4.	To prepare the filling:
5.	Chopped onion - 3
6.	Chopped garlic - 3 cloves
7.	Chopped Green Hot Pepper - 2
8.	Peas - half a cup
9.	Fresh ginger - a teaspoon
10.	Green Coriander - 1 teaspoon
11.	Salt - a teaspoon
12.	Curry - teaspoon
13.	Vegetable oil - two tablespoons
To fry:
1.	Vegetable oil - 2 cups

Steps to Make It:

1.	To prepare the dough: Mix the flour, water, olive oil and salt in a bowl and mix well
        until the ingredients are homogeneous and get a soft dough.
2.	Cover the container with a piece of cloth and set it aside for 15 minutes.
3.	To prepare the filling: Heat the oil over the heat, then add the onions, garlic, potatoes,
        green peppers, peas and ginger and stir the mixture for 5 minutes.
4.	Add to the mixture of coriander, salt and curry and stir well, then remove the mixture from 
        the heat and leave to cool.
5.	Form the dough in circles, stuff it with the samosa mixture, then fold the edges in a triangle 
        shape to close them.
6.	Fry samosas on fire for 15 minutes until golden.
